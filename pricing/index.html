Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a subscription that auto-renews?

No. Every plan is a one-time payment. Nothing renews automatically. When your plan expires, you simply return to the free tier with ads.

Can I use Pro on multiple devices?

Yes! Sign in with the same Google account on any Android device and your Pro status will sync automatically.

What happens if I buy again while Pro is still active?

The new plan's duration is stacked on top of your existing subscription. You never lose a single day you already paid for.

What payment methods are supported?

All major Indian payment methods via Razorpay: UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm, BHIM), credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), net banking, and wallets.

What is the refund policy?

All sales are final. However, if your payment was captured but Pro was not activated, or you were charged twice, email ahmedsameerbinan2@gmail.com within 7 days. See full Terms & Conditions.

Where can I find my payment receipt?

Razorpay sends a receipt to your email/phone. Your payment ID is also visible in the app under Settings → Pro Membership Card.
