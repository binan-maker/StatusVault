Status Saver - WA Vault - Your Privacy is Protected

Status Saver - WA Vault is a fully offline application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

1. Introduction

Status Saver - WA Vault ('we', 'our', 'app') is a WhatsApp Status Saver application for Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. By using Status Saver - WA Vault, you agree to this policy.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

Status Saver - WA Vault does not collect any personal information. We do not collect:

  • Your name, email, or contact information
  • WhatsApp messages, calls, or chats
  • Contacts or phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or analytics
  • Usage statistics or behavior data

All media is processed locally on your device only.

3. Storage Access

Status Saver - WA Vault requires storage access solely to:

  • Read WhatsApp status files from the .Statuses directory
  • Save selected statuses to your device gallery
  • Create a local backup of saved statuses

We only access the WhatsApp .Statuses folder. We cannot and do not access WhatsApp messages, chats, or any other folders.

4. Local Data Storage

Status Saver - WA Vault stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • A list of statuses you have saved (stored in app's private directory)
  • The SAF (Storage Access Framework) folder URI you granted
  • User preferences (stored in app's private storage)

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

5. Advertising

Status Saver - WA Vault displays advertisements via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. This may include:

  • Advertising ID (Android)
  • Device information for ad targeting
  • App usage for frequency capping

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings:
Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

For Google's full privacy policy, visit: policies.google.com/privacy

6. Third-Party Services

Status Saver - WA Vault uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements

All other functionality is performed locally on your device without any network requests.

7. Permissions Explained

READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO:
Required to detect and display WhatsApp statuses from the .Statuses folder.

WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android < 10):
Required to save statuses to the gallery on older devices.

STORAGE ACCESS FRAMEWORK (Android 11+):
Required for the user to manually grant access to the WhatsApp .Statuses folder on newer Android versions.

No permissions are used beyond their stated purpose.
